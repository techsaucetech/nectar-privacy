Privacy Policy for Nectar

Effective Date: March 8, 2025
Last Updated: March 8, 2025

Nectar Technologies, LLC ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the Nectar app.

By using Nectar, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

1.1. Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password.
  • API Keys: If you use the app's analytics features, we collect and store your Beehiiv API key to retrieve newsletter data.
  • Customer Support: If you contact us for support, we may collect your contact details and any information you provide.

1.2. Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the app, such as features used and time spent.
  • Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system, app version, and other technical details.
  • Analytics Data: We use Firebase Analytics to gather aggregated insights about app usage. This data does not personally identify you.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Nectar app.
  • To personalize user experience and improve app performance.
  • To process API requests and display analytics based on your Beehiiv newsletters.
  • To respond to customer support inquiries.
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent activities or security threats.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted partners (such as Firebase) for app functionality and analytics.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, we may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale, your information may be transferred to the new entity.

4. Your Data Choices and Controls

  • Opt-Out of Analytics: Disable Firebase Analytics through your device settings.
  • Delete Your Account: You may request account deletion by contacting hello@nectarstats.com.
  • Revoke API Access: You can remove your Beehiiv API key at any time in the app settings.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Third-Party Services

Nectar integrates with third-party services, including Beehiiv API and Firebase Analytics. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

7. Children’s Privacy

Nectar is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us to request its removal.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify users by updating the Settings page in the app. Your continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
